What is Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (RERA)

RERA is a central legislation that aims to regulate the real estate sector and protect the interests of homebuyers. It mandates the registration of real estate projects and real estate agents, and establishes state-level Real Estate Regulatory Authorities (RERAs) to oversee the implementation of the Act.

Transfer of Property Act, 1882

This law governs the transfer of property in India. It defines various modes of property transfer, including sale, mortgage, lease, and gift. It also lays down the rights and liabilities of both buyers and sellers.



Indian Contract Act, 1872

The Indian Contract Act governs contracts entered into between parties. It specifies the requirements for a valid contract, terms and conditions, performance, breach, and remedies in case of non-compliance.



The Benami Transactions (Prohibition) Act, 1988

The Benami Transactions Act prohibits transactions where a property is held by one person but funded by another, with the intent to hide the beneficial ownership. The Act empowers authorities to confiscate benami properties.
